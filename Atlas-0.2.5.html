Changed

  • The title docks into the top bar as you scroll (iPhone & iPad). While reading a note or browsing All Notes, the large title now slides up and settles into the navigation bar as you scroll down — so you always know where you are — and expands again when you scroll back to the top, matching the native iOS feel.
  • Settings moved to the top bar on iPhone. In All Notes, the Settings gear now lives in the navigation bar alongside the menu button, instead of next to the page heading.

Fixed

  • The formatting bar no longer flickers when you format text (Mac). Applying bold, italic, highlight, or other inline formatting kept the bar steady instead of having it blink out and snap back into place.
  • The formatting bar stays in view for long selections (Mac). Selecting a large block of text now anchors the bar at the top of the selection in view, rather than dropping it to the bottom-left of the editor.
